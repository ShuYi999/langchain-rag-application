Shipping Information and Delivery Times

Standard Shipping:
- Delivery time: 3-5 business days for local deliveries
- Cost: $5.99 for orders under $50
- Free shipping on orders over $50

Express Shipping:
- Delivery time: 1-2 business days
- Cost: $15.99 flat rate
- Available for domestic orders only

International Shipping:
- Delivery time: 7-14 business days
- Cost varies by destination
- Customs fees may apply

Tracking:
- All shipments include tracking numbers
- Tracking information is sent via email within 24 hours of shipment
- Track your order on our website using your order number

Damaged Items:
- Damaged items must be reported within 48 hours of delivery
- Photo documentation required
- Replacement or refund will be issued after review
