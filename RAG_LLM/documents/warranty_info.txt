Product Warranty Coverage

Standard Warranty:
- Warranty coverage lasts for 2 years from the purchase date
- Covers manufacturing defects and material failures
- Does not cover normal wear and tear

Extended Warranty:
- Optional 5-year extended warranty available at purchase
- Adds accidental damage protection
- Includes priority repair service

What's Covered:
- Defective components
- Electrical failures
- Software malfunctions (for electronic devices)

What's Not Covered:
- Physical damage from drops or impacts
- Water damage
- Unauthorized repairs or modifications
- Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect functionality

Warranty Claims:
- Contact customer service with proof of purchase
- Device may need to be sent for inspection
- Repair or replacement decision made within 10 business days
- Free shipping both ways for warranty repairs
